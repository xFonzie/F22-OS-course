Recovery:
It is applicable if the resourse can be taken away safely for the process or the process can be killed or returned to the checkpoint harmlessly.

Prevention:
Is applicable in case one have access to the source code, he might use one of the preventhion techniques. However, often it might be impossible to implement.

Avoidance:
Is applicable if the system have certain information in advance, such as the moment when specific resourse will be requested.
